# **SIMULIA Living Heart Model**

**SIMULIA Living Heart** is a dynamic, organ-level multiphysics simulation of the human heart used for medical device testing and cardiovascular research.

Living Heart typically supports:
- Detailed 4-chamber heart model with electrophysiology and fluid-structure interaction.
- Virtual device implantation for stents, valves, pacemakers, and ECMO.
- Patient-specific customization from medical imaging data.
- Regulatory submission support with in silico clinical trials.

# **MATLAB Medical Device Models**

**MATLAB Medical Device Toolbox** provides Simulink reference models for ventilators, infusion pumps, ECMO, and dialysis systems development.

MATLAB models typically support:
- Virtual prototyping of life-support equipment with control systems.
- Hardware-in-the-loop testing and ISO 60601 compliance verification.
- Artificial pancreas and cardiovascular system simulations.
- Model-based design with automated code generation for embedded devices.

## License and Cost

MathWorks commercial licensing with volume discounts.

## Limitations

Requires MATLAB/Simulink proficiency for customization.

# **COMSOL Multiphysics Biomedical**

**COMSOL Biomedical Models** enable multiphysics simulation of implantable devices, drug delivery systems, and bioelectromagnetics.

COMSOL typically supports:
- Electrodes, neural implants, and bioheat transfer simulations.
- Microfluidics for drug delivery and lab-on-chip devices.
- Tissue mechanics and electromagnetic field interactions.
- Patient-specific organ modeling from MRI/CT data.

## License and Cost

Commercial modular licensing with biomedical add-on modules.

## Limitations

Steep learning curve for multiphysics coupling expertise.

# **Ansys Medical Device Simulator**

**Ansys Medical** provides comprehensive simulation for cardiovascular devices, orthopedics, and wearable health technology development.

Ansys Medical typically supports:
- Stent fatigue, heart valve dynamics, and bone implant analysis.
- CFD for blood flow and respiratory device optimization.
- Electromagnetic compatibility for implants and wearables.
- Virtual human body interaction with device certification support.

## License and Cost

Enterprise Ansys licensing with industry-specific bundles.

## Limitations
